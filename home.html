About My Character

Super Mario is a series of platform video games created by Nintendo featuring their mascot, Mario. Also known as the Super Mario Bros. series or simply the Mario series, it is the main series in the larger Mario franchise. Super Mario has a variety of skills, experiences, and hobbies. As the main character in the Super Mario games, he is known for his jumping ability and various power-ups. Mario also has experience as a plumber, and in the early Donkey Kong games, he was a carpenter. Mario's hobbies include playing sports such as tennis, golf, baseball, soccer, and go-kart racing.
